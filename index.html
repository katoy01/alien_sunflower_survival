ALIEN SUNFOWER SURVIVAL

BUTTONS

KEY (press these keys on your keyboard):

1: equip Sunflower

2: equip Watering Can

W: jump

A: move left

D: move right

S: move down from platform, or while equipping the watering can in your inventory, you can plant sunflower seeds into the ground (you have to wait a whole second for it to finally plant!)

P: shoot sunflower while equipping sunflower in inventory

Description:

You are a cute green alien who landed onto a sunflower field in planet Earth with your private U.F.O. that you 'burrowed' from a friend. While you were examining the humonguous flowers (little did you know you were just super short), you found a small watering can that fit your hand. You learned botany through a book you were reading on your way here so you start planting seeds and growing sunflowers when all of a sudden a hungry hoard of bat enemies storm your way! To protect yourself you pick up the sunflowers you were growing and throw them their way with full force, and to your surprise, it works. The bats become satisfied with the sunflower you give them and disappear from your sight. Now the only way to survive the bat attack is for you to satsify the bats with your homemade sunflowers. Keep growing, harvesting, and throwing sunflowers until you meet your ultimate demise... of being tickled by bats (NO ONE DIES).
